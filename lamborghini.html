About the Lamborghini Urus

The Lamborghini Urus is a luxury SUV that combines sports car performance with the functionality of an SUV. It is equipped with a twin-turbo V8 engine, stunning design, and the most advanced technology. Perfect for those who want power, style, and comfort in one car.

Key Features

  • Engine: 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 641 HP
  • Top Speed: 190 mph (305 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: 3.6 seconds
  • Seating: 5-passenger
